How we Support our Demographic

eDuctu’s goal is to guide the next generation of leaders to financial security. eDuctu provides online financial advice for people that need help with budgeting and managing their finances. In the future we hope to donate 10% of our profits to donations such as Prep for Prep to help underprivileged youth achieve higher education. We want young adults to be financially secure so they could pursue their passions without having to worry about their finances. In order to have an healthy and educated society we must give the next generation room to grow and prosper without being held back by something out of their control.
